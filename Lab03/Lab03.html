Times to pick up donuts

The best times to buy donuts are often early in the morning or late in the afternoon. Early in the morning, many donut shops have just finished baking a fresh batch, so you can enjoy a warm, delicious treat that's still at its peak flavor.
Plus, this timing often means a wider selection and fewer crowds. Late in the afternoon, especially toward closing time, many bakeries offer discounts on remaining stock to avoid waste. This can be a sweet opportunity to snag some great deals, although the selection might be more limited.
